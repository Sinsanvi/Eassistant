# Priority Guidelines

## High Priority Keywords
- urgent
- asap
- deadline
- meeting
- interview
- client
- project delivery
- launch
- critical
- emergency

## Medium Priority Keywords
- follow-up
- review
- sync
- update
- planning
- discussion
- feedback

## Low Priority Keywords
- FYI
- newsletter
- social
- promotional
- optional
- when convenient

## Email Priority Rules
1. Emails from your manager or direct reports are automatically high priority
2. Emails with "deadline" in subject line are high priority
3. Meeting invitations for current/next day are high priority
4. Emails with attachments from clients are medium priority

## Calendar Priority Rules
1. 1-on-1 meetings with manager/direct reports are high priority
2. Client meetings are high priority
3. Project deadlines are high priority
4. Team meetings are medium priority
5. Optional events are low priority

## Meeting Booking Rules
- Only book meetings during working hours (9 AM - 5 PM)
- Leave 15 minutes buffer between meetings
- No back-to-back meetings longer than 2 hours
- Lunch break 12 PM - 1 PM is protected
